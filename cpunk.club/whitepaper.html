2.2 Distribution Model

CPUNK will launch on the Cellframe DEX in 10 distinct orders, each representing 10% of the total supply (100M tokens/order). The initial order is set at 1 CPUNK = 0.0001 CELL, with subsequent orders priced 10% higher than the previous.

3. Governance Model

3.1 Community Proposals

CPUNK embraces decentralized decision-making with a system wherein any holder above a set threshold can submit proposals. During the defined voting window, token holders vote in proportion to their holdings. Proposals that pass are executed on-chain (once Cellframe fully supports complex smart contracts), ensuring trustless implementation.

3.2 Transparency & Accountability

All critical financial and developmental decisions reside on-chain, tied to immutable smart contracts. CPUNK's open-source codebase welcomes ongoing review and collaboration from the community, reinforcing its commitment to accountability and collective governance.
